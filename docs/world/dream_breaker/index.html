Dream Breaker

For complete perks, see d2gunsmith.com, light.gg or destinytracker.com.

Dream Breaker is an Adaptive Frame Solar Fusion Rifle, originally released way back in the Shadowkeep days. It came back to us in S14 with a revamped set of perks that at the time, made for an underwhelming package. In S15, Bungie revamped Fusion Rifles and given the right perks and right playstyle (which I suppose is true for any weapon), the Dream Breaker can put in work.

If you’d like more info on Fusion Rifles, check out Castle’s overview and for Dream Breaker specifically, check out CoolGuy’s recent video.

ðŸ˜Ž CoolGuy’s Pick (pvp)#

Firmly Planted is key to this roll, clearning up all of what ails Fusion Rifles. Kickstart active is what allows us to go all-in on damage reduction trade-off for making Charge Time slower. Arrowhead Brake allows us to hit 100 Recoil with a Counterbalance mod. The benefits from Firmly Planted don’t make this necessary though, hence the suggestion of most any barrel.

  • Collector’s Edition Roll
    • Barrels: Arrowhead Brake
    • Magazines: Accelerated Coils
    • Perks 1: Firmly Planted
    • Perks 2: Kickstart
    • Masterworks: Charge MW
  • Extended Perks (referred to with a ‘+’ below)
    • Barrels: Arrowhead Brake, Polygonal Rifling, Smallbore, Corkscrew Rifling, Fluted Barrel, Chambered Compensator, Hammer-Forged Rifling
    • Magazines: Accelerated Coils, Projection Fuse, Particle Repeater
